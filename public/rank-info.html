📊 System Overview

Cryptexa's Leadership Rank system rewards you for building an active team. Ranks are based on the number of active members (users who have made at least one completed deposit) across your 3-level referral network.

Active Member = Any referral (Level 1, 2, or 3) who has made at least one successful deposit

💰 Referral Commission Structure

Earn commissions from deposits made by your referrals across 3 levels:

Level Description Commission Rate
Level 1 Direct Referrals 2%
Level 2 Referrals of your referrals 1%
Level 3 Third generation referrals 0.5%

🔄 How It Works

1. Build Your Team

  • Share your referral link with others
  • When they register and make deposits, they become active members
  • Active members count across all 3 levels (Level 1 + Level 2 + Level 3)

2. Reach Rank Requirements

  • Your rank is automatically calculated based on total active members
  • Once you reach the requirement, you instantly qualify for that rank
  • Ranks are permanent - you never lose a rank once achieved

3. Receive Rewards

  • One-Time Bonus: Paid immediately when you reach a new rank (paid once per rank)
  • Weekly Salary: Paid every Monday for Elite Leaders and above
  • Referral Commissions: Earned continuously from all deposits

⚠️ Important Information

  • Active Member Definition: A user who has made at least one completed deposit
  • One-Time Bonuses: Paid only once per rank when you first achieve it
  • Weekly Salary: Only Elite Leaders and above receive weekly salary
  • Payment Schedule: Weekly salaries are paid every Monday automatically
  • Rank Permanence: Once you achieve a rank, you keep it forever
  • Commission Timing: Commissions are credited when deposits are completed
  • No Downgrade: Your rank never decreases, even if active members become inactive

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I track my active members?

Visit your Team page to see all your referrals and their status. Active members are marked with a green "Active" badge.

Q: When do I receive my rank bonus?

Rank bonuses are credited to your balance immediately when you reach the required number of active members.

Q: Can I lose my rank?

No! Once you achieve a rank, it's permanent. You'll continue receiving weekly salary even if some members become inactive.

Q: How is weekly salary paid?

Weekly salaries are automatically credited to your balance every Monday. No action required from you.

Q: Do Level 2 and Level 3 members count toward my rank?

Yes! All active members across all 3 levels count toward your total active member count for rank qualification.
